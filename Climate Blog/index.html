Learn About Climate Change

Global Warming

Earth's average temperature has risen by 1.1°C since the late 19th century, mostly due to increased CO2 emissions from human activities.

Rising Sea Levels

Global sea level rose about 8 inches in the last century. The rate in the last two decades is nearly double that of the last century.

Extreme Weather

Climate change has led to more frequent and intense extreme weather events like hurricanes, heatwaves, and heavy rainfall.

About Climate Voices

Our Mission

Climate Voices empowers young people to express their concerns about climate change through creative musical expression. We believe art and music can inspire action where facts alone may fail.

By combining environmental education with music composition, we're creating a movement of informed, passionate advocates for our planet's future.
